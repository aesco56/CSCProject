How to Play

This game is a reaction point and click game.
The objective is simple look for the correct object and click it as fast as you can.

The game will display objects and have a prompt.
Your objective is to distingush the right object and avoid objects that do not fit the description.

Correct objects will increase your score by 1.
If you happen to click on the wrong object, your score will decrease by 1.
The game will continue until the timer runs out.
